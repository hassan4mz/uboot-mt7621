Calibration Data Update

You are going to update Calibration Data on the device.
Please, choose the file from your local hard drive and click Update Calibration Data button.

WARNINGS
  • Do not power off the device during the update.
  • If everything goes well, the device will restart automatically.
  • Make sure you upload the correct Calibration Data image for your MediaTek device.
